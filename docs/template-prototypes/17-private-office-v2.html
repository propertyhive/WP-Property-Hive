The House

Rooms that keep their own company

Waterman Court asks nothing of its owner but attention to the light. Remodelled in 2023, the house gives every principal room a garden aspect, so the day moves through it — morning in the kitchen beneath the roof lanterns, evening in the sitting room as the beeches take the last sun.

Sitting Room 24'1" × 16'4" (7.34m × 4.98m) Facing south-west across the terrace through full-height glazing, with a limestone fireplace and oak boards throughout.

Kitchen / Dining Room 28'6" × 15'9" (8.69m × 4.80m) The working heart of the 2023 remodel, running the full depth of the plan beneath twin roof lanterns; island breakfast bar and a garden-end table for twelve.

Principal Bedroom Suite 18'2" × 14'6" (5.54m × 4.42m) Glazed doors straight onto the terrace, dressing area and a marble-lined shower room.

Bedrooms Two & Three 14'8" × 12'1" · 12'6" × 11'4" Both doubles, both garden-facing, each with its own bath or shower room.

Garden & Studio South-west facing Stone terrace off the kitchen, lawn framed by mature beech, and a detached, insulated studio at the boundary — currently a home office.
