Description

Waterman Court is a small private close on Wokingham's favoured south-west side, and number 91 holds its quietest corner. The house was comprehensively remodelled in 2023 around a single idea: every principal room faces the garden.

The kitchen and dining space runs the full depth of the plan beneath two roof lanterns, with a separate sitting room for the evening. Three double bedrooms each carry their own bath or shower room — rare at this price in a single-storey home.
