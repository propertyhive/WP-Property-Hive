Chapter 01

Arrival

Waterman Court is a private close of six houses, and number 91 holds the head of it — no through traffic, beech trees behind, and a low brick line that gives nothing away from the road.

Garden & studio

Landscaped in the same 2023 works: a stone terrace off the kitchen, lawn framed by mature beech, and a detached studio at the boundary — wired, insulated, and currently a home office.

Chapter 06

The area

Ten minutes' walk to the market square, less to the woods. Wokingham station is 0.8 miles — Waterloo in under an hour, Reading in ten minutes — and the town's Outstanding-rated schools are the reason many families stop looking once they get here.
