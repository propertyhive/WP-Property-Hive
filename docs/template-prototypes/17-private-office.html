Offers over £1,340,000  ·  Bungalow  ·  3 bed · 3 bath · 2 reception

A single-storey house arranged entirely around its garden, at the quiet head of a private court.

The House

Rooms that keep their own company

Waterman Court asks nothing of its owner but attention to the light. Remodelled in 2023, the house gives every principal room a garden aspect, so the day moves through it — morning in the kitchen beneath the roof lanterns, afternoon on the terrace, evening in the drawing room as the beeches take the last sun.

Three double bedrooms each hold their own bath or shower room, the principal suite opening directly to the garden. A detached studio at the boundary serves as office, gym or guest annexe as the household requires.

The Setting

Wokingham, at a remove

The town's market square is a ten-minute walk, the station scarcely further — Waterloo in under an hour, Reading in ten minutes. Yet the court itself is a cul-de-sac of six houses, and number 91 sits at its head with woodland bridleways beginning four hundred yards from the gate.
